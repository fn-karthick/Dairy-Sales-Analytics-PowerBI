# Dairy Sales Analytics Dashboard | Power BI

## Project Overview

The **Dairy Sales Analytics Dashboard** is an interactive Business Intelligence solution developed using **Microsoft Power BI** to analyze dairy sales, revenue trends, inventory performance, and regional business insights.

The dashboard transforms raw transactional data into meaningful visualizations that enable businesses to monitor KPIs, identify high-performing products, evaluate sales channels, and optimize inventory management through data-driven decision-making.

## Project Objectives

- Analyze overall business revenue and sales performance
- Monitor product-wise sales and profitability
- Compare the performance of different sales channels
- Identify top-performing brands and customer locations
- Track inventory levels and reorder requirements
- Discover key business drivers using interactive analytics

# Dataset

This project uses the **Dairy Goods Sales Dataset** available on Kaggle.

### Dataset Description

The dataset contains transactional information from dairy farms and dairy product sales. It includes details related to products, brands, sales channels, inventory, customer locations, farm information, and revenue.

### Dataset Includes

- Product Name
- Brand
- Location
- Customer Location
- Farm Size
- Land Area
- Number of Cows
- Quantity Produced
- Quantity Sold
- Revenue
- Inventory Metrics
- Sales Channel
- Date

**Kaggle Dataset:**

The Dairy Goods Sales Dataset provides a detailed and comprehensive collection of data related to dairy farms, dairy products, sales, and inventory management. This dataset encompasses a wide range of information, including farm location, land area, cow population, farm size, production dates, product details, brand information, quantities, pricing, shelf life, storage conditions, expiration dates, sales information, customer locations, sales channels, stock quantities, stock thresholds, and reorder quantities.

**https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/suraj520/dairy-goods-sales-dataset**
